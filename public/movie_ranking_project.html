The Best Documentaries According to Linus

My Top 3 Documentaries of All-Time

The Mind, Explained

This documentary explores the brain's inner workings, like memory and dreams, in accessible detail.

Minimalism: A Documentary About the Important Things

This documentary emphasizes living intentionally by valuing experiences and relationships over possessions.

Tony Robbins: I Am Not Your Guru

Viewers witness powerful life transformations as Tony Robbins helps people overcome personal challenges.
